Presentation of the training


The IT departments of IUT de France train students in IT and teamwork in order to integrate them into professional life.

They thus offer training on a human scale, supervised by available teachers, as well as by experienced professionals who provide students with practical and concrete experience in IT professions.

Computer science education (DUT or Professional License) is based on 3 axes, each of them requiring specific qualities and skills:

Analysis and design

Preliminary work in relation with the client to specify his needs and expectations and the design of the diagram of a computer processing.

Communication, open-mindedness, analytical and synthesis skills ...

Algorithmic and programming

Effective implementation of processing up to coding.

Logical mind, technical precision ...

Operation of a computer, its operating system and networks.

Operation of a computer, its operating system and networks (Internet, Intranet, ...).

curiosity, patience ...

Half of these targeted courses are general education disciplines (Mathematics, Economics and Management, English and Communication, etc.). This gives the training a multidisciplinary aspect.

In addition, the evaluation is done not only on theory but also on scenarios, via tutored projects and a work placement, allowing to validate the knowledge and skills acquired during the training.
