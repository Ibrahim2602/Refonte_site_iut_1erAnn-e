University Institutes Of Technology

The University Institutes of Technology in France are spread over the whole territory and number 115, which ensures a strong anchoring in the local and regional economic environment. Meetings between department directors of IUTs ensure national consistency in teaching.

The IUT has a university teaching quality recognized and acclaimed by companies for more than 40 years, thanks to the establishment of programs adapted to professional reality and the creation of diplomas responding to the emergence of new professions.

The courses covering most sectors (secondary and tertiary) and offering the collaboration of professionals are the strength and success of IUT, and in fact allow students to find a job within 6 months of the award of the diploma.

This diversification of diplomas and training leads to the implementation of different teaching methods, which thus suit a greater number of students and professionals wishing to resume studies or to carry out a validation of acquired experience (VAE).

In addition, IUTs, in partnership with foreign universities, offer further study abroad as well as internships.

IT departments at the IUT

The teaching of computer science at the IUT gives a large part to the professional dimension while retaining fundamental components; the student thus acquires know-how but also knowledge, basic concepts and working methods that he will be able to use in a professional situation.

The DUT, university and professional diploma (level L2 of the LMD scheme, 120 European credits):

Holders of the DUT in computer science are senior technicians, capable of participating in the design, construction and implementation of computer systems. They can, however, if they wish, continue their studies at the end of this diploma.

The Professional License "Computer Systems and Software" (SIL), level L3 diploma in the LMD scheme (License degree, 180 European credits): these pages present the professional licenses offered in the IT departments of IUT

SIL license holders are specialists capable of participating in a software development project and ultimately taking full responsibility for it.

The validation of acquired knowledge:

It is also possible to have one's experience recognized (professional or not) in order to obtain a diploma, a title or a professional qualification certificate. You can visit the government site for validation of acquired experience (VAE).
