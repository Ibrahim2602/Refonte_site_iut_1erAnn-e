Testimony of Damien Lefebvre

His bac S in his pocket, Damien Lefebvre spent a few months on the benches of the university, before reorienting himself, in search of a more supervised training. His university experience having revealed to him a pronounced taste for IT, he decided to apply to the IUT of Villetaneuse in order to prepare a DUT in IT, option Software Engineering in 2002.

The supervision offered by passionate teachers, the acquisition of rigorous working methods encouraged him to complete his training via the Professional License in Computer Systems and Software option Software Engineering, offered by the same IUT.

"The knowledge and skills developed by three years of studying algorithms, databases, but also accounting aspects of the business world, were the key that allowed me to be recruited by a large French IT service company, after 4 months of end-of-year internship "

"For a year and a half now, the young Analyst Programmer has been putting his skills at the service of major clients, including renowned banking establishments, and wishes in the future to move towards Consulting"
